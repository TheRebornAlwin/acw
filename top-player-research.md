## CROSS-INDUSTRY ANALYSIS

### What's Consistent Across the Best Performers

1. **Clear, benefit-led headlines** — Never "Welcome to our website." Always about the customer's outcome (kerb appeal, property protection, hassle-free service).

2. **Before/after imagery is NON-NEGOTIABLE** — Every single top performer uses before/after photos. This is the #1 visual trust element in the industry.

3. **Multiple contact methods** — Phone, email, online form, and increasingly WhatsApp. The best sites offer 3-4 ways to get in touch.

4. **Strong guarantee language** — "Satisfied or it's free," "100% guarantee," "We'll come back within 3 days." Risk reversal is standard at the top.

5. **Google reviews prominently displayed** — Not hidden on a separate page. Embedded on the homepage with star ratings visible.

6. **Named team members** — Customers want to know WHO is coming to their property. Personal names and photos build trust.

7. **Free quote as primary CTA** — Every top performer leads with a free, no-obligation quote. This is the universal conversion mechanism.

8. **Local SEO pages** — Top local operators create individual pages for each town/area they serve.

9. **Service-specific pages** — Each service gets its own dedicated page with unique content, not just a list on one page.

---

### Design Patterns That Work Best

| Pattern | Why It Works |
|---------|-------------|
| **Hero slider with before/after** | Instant visual proof of capability |
| **4-step "How It Works" section** | Reduces anxiety, sets expectations |
| **Service cards in 3-4 column grid** | Easy scanning, professional layout |
| **Sticky header with phone number** | Always-accessible contact |
| **Testimonial carousel** | Social proof without taking too much space |
| **Trust badges row** | Quick credibility scan (Google rating, certifications, years in business) |
| **Blue + white colour scheme** | Communicates cleanliness, professionalism, trust |
| **CTA repeated every 2-3 scroll sections** | Catches users at different decision points |
| **Mobile-first responsive design** | 60%+ of traffic is mobile for local services |

### Emotional Triggers Used

1. **Pride of Ownership** — "Your home deserves to look its best." Appeals to homeowners' pride in their property.

2. **Fear of Deterioration** — "Dirt and grime silently destroy property value." Creates urgency by highlighting costs of inaction.

3. **Social Proof / Belonging** — "Join 500+ happy customers in [area]." Nobody wants to be the only one with dirty windows on the street.

4. **Convenience / Time Saving** — "Reclaim your weekends." Positions the service as lifestyle enhancement, not just cleaning.

5. **First Impressions / Curb Appeal** — "94% of buyers form opinions within 30 seconds." Powerful for homeowners considering selling.

6. **Trust / Safety** — "Fully insured, vetted, and certified." Addresses the fear of letting strangers onto your property.

7. **Transformation / Satisfaction** — Before/after images trigger the desire for that same transformation. Visual storytelling at its best.

---

### How Top Players Handle Gallery/Portfolio

- **Before/after sliders** (interactive drag-to-reveal) are the gold standard
- **Location-tagged project case studies** (Vision Exterior) — shows you work in the customer's area
- **Instagram feed embeds** (JP Exterior) — always fresh, always updating, feels real
- **Categorised galleries** — separated by service type (windows, driveways, roofs, gutters)
- **Brief descriptions with each project** — not just photos, but context ("3-bed semi in [town], hadn't been cleaned in 5 years")
- Top performers NEVER use stock photos — authenticity is critical (real photos convert 35% better)

---

### How Top Players Handle Reviews/Testimonials

- **Volume over perfection** — Impact shows 100+ reviews; Fantastic Cleaners shows 49,000+
- **Google reviews widget embedded on homepage** — not a separate page
- **Star ratings always visible** — the visual of 5 gold stars is universally understood
- **Named reviewers with photos where possible** — adds authenticity
- **Service-specific testimonials** — reviews mentioning the exact service the visitor is browsing
- **Third-party platform badges** — Trustpilot, Google, Checkatrade, Yell logos
- **Video testimonials** (when available) outperform written ones
- Reviews consistently mention: punctuality, politeness, quality of result, value for money

## ACTIONABLE RECOMMENDATIONS FOR AC WINDOWS & CLEANING SERVICES

### Priority 1: Must-Have Elements

1. **Hero Section Formula:**
   - Headline: Benefit-driven, location-specific. Example: "Barry's Most Trusted Window & Exterior Cleaning Service" or "Sparkling Windows, Pristine Driveways — Guaranteed."
   - Sub-headline: Address the core problem or desire
   - CTA: "Get Your Free Quote" (large, prominent button)
   - Background: Before/after slider or professional action shot
   - Trust bar underneath: Google rating + years in business + "Fully Insured" + number of happy customers

2. **Before/After Interactive Slider** — This is the single highest-impact visual element. Get professional before/after photos of real jobs (especially dramatic driveway transformations and window cleaning on period properties).

3. **Google Reviews Widget** — Embed live Google reviews on the homepage. Aim for 50+ reviews. After every job, send a follow-up text/WhatsApp with a direct Google review link.

4. **"How It Works" Section** — 3-4 steps with icons:
   - Step 1: Request Your Free Quote (phone, WhatsApp, or online form)
   - Step 2: We Arrange a Time That Suits You
   - Step 3: We Clean to Perfection
   - Step 4: You Inspect — 100% Satisfaction Guaranteed

5. **Service-Specific Pages** — Individual pages for: Window Cleaning, Driveway & Patio Cleaning, Gutter Cleaning, Fascia & Soffit Cleaning, Conservatory Cleaning, Pressure Washing. Each with unique content, photos, pricing guidance, and FAQs.

6. **Satisfaction Guarantee** — "Not happy? We'll come back and re-clean for free within 48 hours." This is now table stakes in the industry.

### Priority 2: High-Impact Additions

7. **WhatsApp Integration** — Floating WhatsApp button: "Tap for a Quick Free Quote." This is increasingly the preferred contact method, especially for under-45 homeowners.

8. **Local Area Pages** — Create SEO-optimised pages for: Barry, Penarth, Dinas Powys, Sully, Rhoose, Llantwit Major, Cowbridge, Cardiff, Vale of Glamorgan. Each page should mention local landmarks and specific neighbourhoods.

9. **Named Team with Photos** — "Meet the AC Windows Team." Show real faces. Mention experience, qualifications, and something personal. Customers want to know who's coming to their home.

10. **Instant Quote Calculator** — Even a simple one: "Select your property type > How many bedrooms? > Which services? > Get instant estimate." This dramatically reduces friction.

11. **Instagram/Social Feed Embed** — Post every job's before/after to Instagram, embed the feed on your website. Creates a continuously updated portfolio.

### Priority 3: Differentiation Opportunities

12. **"Small Business, Big Results" Positioning** — Like Impact Exterior Cleaning. Own the local, personal, family-feel angle. You're not a faceless corporation — you know your customers by name.

13. **Eco-Friendly Messaging** — Purified water systems, biodegradable products, responsible waste handling. This is a growing differentiator, especially in Wales where environmental awareness is high.

14. **Seasonal Promotions** — Christmas light installation (like Shine), spring clean packages, "New Year New Shine" campaigns. Creates recurring touchpoints.

15. **Blog/Content Section** — "How often should you clean your gutters in Barry?" / "Why South Wales weather makes regular window cleaning essential." Builds SEO authority and positions AC Windows as the local expert.

### Colour & Design Recommendations

- **Primary colour:** A strong blue (#0066CC or similar) — communicates trust, cleanliness, professionalism
- **Accent colour:** A vibrant green or teal — freshness, eco-friendliness, the Welsh landscape
- **Warning/CTA colour:** Orange or amber for buttons — high visibility, urgency without aggression
- **Background:** Clean white with light grey sections for visual separation
- **Typography:** Sans-serif, modern, highly readable (e.g., Inter, Poppins, or Montserrat)
- **Photography:** Exclusively real photos from actual jobs. No stock photos whatsoever.

### Copy Tone Recommendation

Based on the best performers, AC Windows should adopt:
- **Confident but not arrogant** — "We take pride in every pane" not "We're the best"
- **Friendly and local** — Reference Barry, the Vale, South Wales. Feel like a neighbour, not a stranger
- **Outcome-focused** — Talk about how the customer will feel, not just what you do
- **Specific** — "Your windows will be streak-free and sparkling" not "We clean windows"
- **Reassuring** — Address objections proactively: "Fully insured. No mess. No fuss. Guaranteed."

## SUMMARY: THE WINNING FORMULA

The best exterior cleaning websites follow this proven structure:

```
HEADER: Logo | Phone Number | "Get a Free Quote" button
HERO: Benefit headline + before/after visual + primary CTA + trust indicators
HOW IT WORKS: 3-4 step process with icons
SERVICES: Grid of service cards with photos and brief descriptions
SOCIAL PROOF: Google reviews widget + testimonial carousel
ABOUT/TEAM: Named team members with photos + years of experience
GALLERY: Before/after slider + categorised project photos
SERVICE AREAS: Map or list of towns covered
FAQ: Common questions answered (great for SEO)
FINAL CTA: "Ready for sparkling results? Get your free quote today."
FOOTER: Contact details, service list, area list, social links, certifications
```

The companies that win are not necessarily the biggest — they're the ones that:
1. Make it effortless to get a quote
2. Prove their quality visually (before/after)
3. Build trust through reviews and guarantees
4. Feel local and personal, not corporate
5. Show up first in local search results

AC Windows & Cleaning Services has every opportunity to build a website that competes with — and outperforms — these top players at the local level.
