What We Do

Every Surface, Spotless

Whether it's streaky windows driving you mad, a driveway that's turned into a slip hazard, or gutters overflowing every time it rains — we sort it. Properly.

Window Cleaning

Crystal-clear windows that actually let the light in. We use purified water-fed pole systems for a streak-free finish that lasts, covering every window, frame, and sill — inside and out. The kind of clean where you walk into the room and genuinely notice the difference in how bright everything feels.

Pressure Washing

Driveways, patios, paths, decking — we strip away years of grime, moss, and algae. The difference is night and day. No surface damage, no mess left behind.

Gutter Cleaning

Blocked gutters cause real damage — overflowing water saturates walls, breeds mould, and costs thousands to fix. We clear them out completely.

Roof Cleaning

Moss damages tiles and shortens your roof's lifespan. We remove it safely, treat the surface to slow regrowth, and leave it looking years younger.

Driveway Cleaning

That green, slippery mess covering your driveway? Gone. We'll have it looking like the day it was laid. No harsh chemicals, no puddles — just clean.

About Us

Meet Anthony

AC Windows & Cleaning Services started because Anthony got tired of hearing the same story from neighbours and friends across Barry — unreliable cleaners, shoddy work, and no-shows. Everyone had a horror story about a window cleaner who just vanished, or a pressure washer who left the place worse than before.

"If you want something done right, do it yourself. So that's exactly what I did."

He built a service around the one thing nobody else seemed to get right: actually showing up, doing a proper job, and treating people's homes with respect. No corporate runaround. Just a local bloke who lives in the community, knows the streets, and takes genuine pride in making your home look its absolute best.

Every job gets the same care — whether it's a quick window clean on a terraced house or a full exterior transformation. Anthony and his team arrive on time, communicate clearly, and won't leave until the job's done right.
