**Date:** 20 February 2026
**Market:** Window cleaning, exterior cleaning, pressure washing -- Barry / Vale of Glamorgan / Cardiff, Wales

## COMPETITOR 1: Barry Window Cleaners

**Website:** https://barrywindowcleaners.co.uk/

- **Services:** Window cleaning, fascia cleaning, gutter cleaning
- **Headlines:** "Premier local domestic window cleaning service in the Vale of Glamorgan" + "Fully insured, reliable and trustworthy."
- **Tone:** Professional yet approachable and friendly. Emphasises local roots, reliability, and safety.
- **Mechanisms:** "Latest reach and wash purified water systems" -- calls out pole-based systems as eliminating "clanging ladders"
- **Positioning:** Family-run, local Barry business. AXA public liability insurance named specifically. Uniformed staff.
- **CTA:** "GET A QUOTE" and "BOOK NOW" + WhatsApp contact
- **Winning Tactic:** Naming their insurance provider (AXA) rather than generic "fully insured" adds specificity and believability.

## COMPETITOR 2: SMS Window Cleaning

**Website:** https://smswindowcleaning.com/

- **Services:** Window cleaning, gutter cleaning, soffit/fascia, solar panel, conservatory, exterior cleaning
- **Headlines:** "Specialists In Window Cleaning & Gutter Cleaning" + "More than just window cleaners -- we are your neighbours!"
- **Tone:** Warm, community-focused, family-owned feel

- **Winning Tactic:** FAQ section that pre-handles objections. Most competitors do not address buyer hesitations at all.

## COMPETITOR 3: Cardiff Window Cleaner Ltd

**Website:** https://www.cardiffwindowcleanerltd.co.uk/

- **Services:** Widest range -- window cleaning, gutter/uPVC, pressure washing, soft washing, roof cleaning, render/stone/brick, graffiti removal, solar panels, high-rise, conservatory
- **Headlines:** "Specialist Cleaning Services For Cardiff & South Wales" + "Working for a Cleaner Cardiff since 1978."
- **Tone:** Professional and authoritative with heavy before/after imagery
- **Mechanisms:** Standard pressure washing AND soft washing as distinct processes
- **Positioning:** Most established player. Safe Contractor Approved. B2B and B2C.
- **Winning Tactic:** Before/after case study pages with location-specific names. Gold standard for trust + local SEO.

## COMPETITOR 4: Advanced External Cleaning Ltd

**Website:** https://advancedexternalcleaning.com/

- **Services:** Render cleaning, roof cleaning, gutter cleaning, driveway/patio, soft washing, commercial
- **Headlines:** "Cardiff's Premier Exterior Cleaning Specialists" + "Perfect 10/10 on Checkatrade"
- **Tone:** Professional but personal. Father-and-son story creates warmth.
- **Mechanisms:** STRONGEST mechanism-led competitor. Clear six-step process. Soft washing uses "75% less water." Mentions "coastal air and weather" causing algae -- local expertise framing.

- **Winning Tactic:** Named, numbered process (six steps) is the most sophisticated marketing mechanism in this market. Photo documentation follow-up is a brilliant retention tool.

## CROSS-COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

### Universal Claims (Table Stakes)
- "Fully insured" -- every competitor
- "Free quotes" -- universal
- "Professional and reliable" -- most overused words
- "Water-fed pole system / purified water" -- commoditised
- "Local" -- everyone claims it

### Market Sophistication: Stage 2-3
- Stage 2 (enlarged claims) = where most competitors sit
- Stage 3 (mechanism/unique process) = where leading edge is moving
- **Opportunity:** Enter Stage 3-4 with named proprietary process + visual proof
